### ✨ About Metafori

Born from [Lab.SNG](https://lab.sng.sk/) (the Slovak National Gallery’s former in-house digital team), we bring over a decade of experience creating open digital tools for culture. We are ready to bring your ideas to life – from the first spark all the way through launch and beyond.

* **We handle your whole project:** Curators, designers, and developers working as one.
* **We already speak your language:** We understand the nuance of collection care, exhibition planning, and audience behavior.
* **We build to launch and sustain:** Whether you need a platform that lasts for years or something up and running in weeks.
* **Online collections, done right:** For over a decade, we’ve built and cared for the largest art catalogue in Central Europe, and we use those insights to build platforms that are flexible, accessible, and open to discovery.

*Let's build something together!*
